What are anagrams?

An anagram is the rearrangement of letters in a word. For example, 'cat' would be an anagram of 'act', because rearranging the letters of one gives you the other.

How to play

On this website, you will find a new anagram puzzle to solve every day. All you have to do to solve them is rearrange the mixed up words to make a real word. Once you have found the word, you can submit it to check if it's correct.
